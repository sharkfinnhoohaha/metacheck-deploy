### 1. [HIGH · partial] Everything runs in your browser — your unreleased music never leaves your device.
- **Category:** privacy · first-pass: partial→partial (refuted)
- **Evidence:** I tried to refute the first auditor and could not. The first clause "Everything runs in your browser" is false; the second clause is technically narrow-true only in the demo.

CONFIRMED off-device flows that break "Everything":

No gating, on-device-inference path, or disclaimer exists anywhere in app/, lib/, or config that rescues the word "Everything."

Replace "Everything runs in your browser — your unreleased music never leaves your device." with something like: "Validation and artwork QC run 100% in your browser — your metadata only leaves your device if you ask for an AI fix or save a release." The honest, scoped wording already exists in two places in the same repo (features page + privacy page), so this is a one-line copy change, not a code change.
